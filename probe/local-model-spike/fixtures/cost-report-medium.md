# Quarterly Operations Report

This report summarizes operations for the third quarter. It was prepared by the operations team and reviewed by finance.

## Executive Summary

Overall performance was strong, though several figures require review before sign-off. The labor cost figure is $42,500 for the quarter. Revenue grew by 12% compared to the prior quarter.

## Cost Summary

The labor cost figure is $42,500 for the quarter, which appears inconsistent with the stated invoice total of $40,000. Material costs were $18,200. Overhead was reported at $9,750.

The report was finalized on March 3, 2026, after two rounds of review.

## Headcount

The team grew from 14 to 17 people during the quarter. Three engineers and one designer were hired. Attrition remained low.

## Vendor Notes

Our primary vendor raised prices in February. The contract renewal is due on March 3, 2026. We should negotiate before signing.

## Risks

Supply chain delays remain the largest risk. A secondary risk is the labor cost figure, which is still unreconciled. Customer concentration is moderate.

## Recommendations

We recommend finalizing the budget after reconciling the labor cost figure. The team should also review vendor pricing. No other changes are needed at this time.
